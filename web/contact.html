FAQ

Q. My game list is not updating

This site updates Epic Games Store data daily. Try clearing your browser cache or refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R).

Q. How do I claim a free game?

Click the "View on Epic Store" button to open the game page. Log in to your Epic Games account and add the game to your library.

Q. The free game info seems incorrect

All data comes from official Epic Games Store sources. If something looks wrong, please share details by email.
